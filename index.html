About

Ahmed Bilal is a creative technologist whose work inhabits the glitching threshold between cybernetic systems and sensorial experience. Merging ambient design with speculative fiction, their practice explores the latent narratives of machines, oscillating between poetic code and pulsing hardware. From server-stack light choreography to proximity-reactive installations, their projects evoke a technoccult sensibility: animating circuits not as tools, but as agents of estranged perception.
